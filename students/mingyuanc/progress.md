### Lead & Technical Contributions

* **V9 Migration (Clean slate):** Lead and coordinated the effort to deprecate the google datastore to allow for a full SQL driven approach. At the end of the project, we had a functioning staging server as well as code that was well tested.
* **V9 Migration (testing):** Guide and providing technical support to CS3281 students migrating our tests suites. In this way I was able to unblock them but also teach them how to unblock themselves
* **MS Entra:** Explored alternative OAuth2 providers such as MS Entra as well as Okta. We also prototyped adopting the OIDC standards instead of being tailored into a single provider
* **Deprecating GoogleId** As part of the feature to support MS Entra, we decided to do away with google OAuth2 and google specific terminology. This change includes modernizing of the database, authentication flow and even the certain application logic

Special thanks to all the CS3281 students who worked tirelessly on this application as well as the in industry mentors who took time out of their busy schedule to mentor not only us but the CS3281 students as well.
