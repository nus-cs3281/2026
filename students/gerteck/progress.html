Summary

Over the semester, I have been working on leading the dev team for MarkBind. This includes PR reviews, weekly team discussions, bug fixes, releases, and other tasks. My primary technical focus has been on TypeScript migration, ESM transition, and architectural refactoring of the Core and CLI packages to improve maintainability.

Lead & Technical Contributions
